Annual Report Extract - FY2025

Major products: industrial adhesives, specialty coating compounds.
Principal raw materials: petrochemical derivatives, solvents, packaging film.

The company sources inputs from a broad domestic supplier base and no single supplier accounts for more than 18% of purchases.
Management notes commodity prices remained volatile during the year and input costs were partly commodity-linked.

Customer mix remains diversified across OEM, distribution, and export channels.
Top customer contributed 14% of annual revenue.
No material delayed payments were observed and collection period remained stable at 62 days.

There is no significant import dependency in raw-material procurement.

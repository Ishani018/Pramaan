Annual Report Extract - FY2025

Major products: woven sacks and flexible packaging.
Principal raw materials: polymer granules, inks, and additives.

The company has key customers in agro-distribution and local retail chains.
Top 2 customers account for 68% of total revenue, indicating high customer concentration.
Sales are routed through small buyers and local traders in several regions.

Trade receivables increased sharply during the year.
Receivable days increased from 74 to 102.
The management discussion notes delayed payments from key customers and slow collections in Q4.

Raw materials are procured from multiple domestic vendors and no major supplier concentration is reported.
